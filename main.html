My Undergraduate Thesis

Electrical Engineering

18 August 2021
Click here for more details

Description

My thesis/final project was taken from the problem of the covid-19 virus that spread widely in Indonesia in 2021 which resulted in the government limiting people's activities to leave the house and requiring all people to apply standard health protocols such as wearing face masks, implementing physical and social distancing, avoid crowds, and wash hands whenever necessary.

Because of this, people who leave their homes and who will enter public spaces will be screened which is usually still done manually using workers and thermal guns, therefore it was thought to make an automatic covid-19 screening tool called Aftercam Video (Automatic Face Tracking Camera for Covid-19 Detection).

My thesis discusses the field of automatic face mask recognition from the image captured by the tool to determine whether a person is wearing or not wearing a face mask properly, entitled "Implementation of an Automatic Face Mask Recognition System Using the Application of the MobileNetV2-Based CNN Algorithm on the Automatic Face Tracking Camera for Covid-19 Detection (AFTERCAM VIDEO)".
